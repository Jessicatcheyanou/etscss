Cameroon Safety Services(CSS)

About US

CAMEROON SAFETY SERVICES (CSS) is a consulting firm specialize in providing international Standard Corporate Safety and QHSE related services. Our main goal is to “make business World a Healthy, Safe and Eco-Friendly Environment"

Porttitor
Our Services/Products

CSS was incorporated to assist Enterprises secure people, properties and the Environment by preventing work related dangers, accidents, injuries and Environmental pollution using QHSE (Quality, Health, Safety and Environment) Management systems.

  • Corporate Health and Safety Training

    Our multitude of Safety trainings range from Commercial to industrial Safety usually in the form of a work shop.

  • QHSE Consulting

    Our consulting services performed by very experience QHSE Engineers include,

    1. Etude de Danger (EDD) et Plan d’Urgence / Risk assessment and emergency plan
    2. Environmental Impact Assessment and Social Consultation (EIAS)/ Etude d’impact Environmental (EIE).

  • GREEN SPACE MANAGEMENT SERVICES

    Plants play an important role in regulating global Climate. Reduction in Plants will have an impact on our Climate and result in an increase in Global Temperature termed as “Global Warming”.

  • Green Space Creation

    CSS can assist you in Designing and creating a green space around your house and business area. The plants are carefully selected .in order to give your environment a pleasant and natural look.

  • Gardeners Services

    Our Gardeners will provide you with the necessary assistance needed to maintain your environment Clean and Green all year through

  • WASTE MANAGEMENT

    We conscious that many companies’ activities produce a lot of waste and are concern about disposing of them, do not worry we provide you with environmentally friendly waste management system and proper disposal mechanism as per National and International Norms and regulations.

  • ECO-CLEANING

    We offer a very professional and even some days for a free trial Cleaning services to Corporates because we are best at practice. Our Janitors are trained with a Safety conscious cleaning philosophy which enables them to professionally keep your facility clean, fresh and safe all the time using Eco-friendly products

  • GEO ENGINEEERING

    Our engineers provide your project with geological survey, analysis and advice base on their many years of accumulated experience and mastery of Cameroon soil profile.
